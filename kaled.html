I'm Abdul Khaled Mohebi, but you can call me Khaled Jawan. Born in 1994 in a beautiful village in Kabul, I initially pursued civil engineering in university. However, my passion for computer science led me to switch majors.

During my university days, I discovered my knack for sketching and painting. To cover my expenses, I worked as a graphic designer. It was a thrilling time, balancing my studies and honing my design skills.

Later, I landed a fantastic opportunity as an Android developer at a renowned company. It was there that I realized my potential as a UI/UX designer. Over three years, I worked on exciting projects in education, payment systems, online shops, and various management applications, infusing them with a touch of my magic.

But that's not all! Alongside my design journey, I also ventured into the world of competitive programming. In 2021, I achieved great success as a coach for the World Finals of ICPC in Moscow, where I guided and mentored aspiring coding champions.

Now, based in Germany, I'm ready to bring my creative ideas to life and shine as a UI/UX designer.

Join me on this incredible journey, and together, let's create something truly amazing!
